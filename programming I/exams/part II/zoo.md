# **Zoo management system**  

## **Make a zoo management system with the following data**  

### **Animal data (_struct_)**  

* Species (_Array of minimum 5 species. e.g: Feline, canine, etc._)  
* Name (_e.g: White Tiger_)
* Weight  
* Height  
* Sex (_M or F_)  
* Quantity
